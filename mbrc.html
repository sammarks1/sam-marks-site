MBRC

A framework for a flourishing life.

After two decades of building, investing, and traveling, Sam found that success in one area rarely produces a full life on its own. MBRC is the framework he uses to keep four things in balance — and the foundation of the community he's building.

Mind

Clarity, learning, and inner development. The discipline of attention and the practice of thinking well.

Body

Health, energy, and longevity. The physical foundation everything else is built on.

Roots

Relationships, community, and belonging — and a place to recharge. The people and spaces you return to for renewal.

Capital

Not just finance, but resourcefulness. The ability to create, grow, and reach outward — with wealth as one tool among many. Wealth creates freedom; wisdom determines how that freedom is used.

The Build Mastermind brings this framework to life with a small group of founders, investors, and builders.
